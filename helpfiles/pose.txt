pose
Sends a phrase to the room describing your actions
Use '.' to signify a verb to translated. Pronouns will be automatically translated unless a '\' is placed before them. e.g. '.mumble "\I'm sorry for this."'

Syntax
pose <phrase>
.<verb> (phrase)

Aliases
.<verb> .

Example
.doff my hat
yields "{name} doffs {pronouns.my} hat"

pose .walk towards my horse. After a moment, I .stop, and .look at myself.
yields "{name} walks towards {pronouns.my} horse. After a moment, {pronouns.i} stops and looks at {pronouns.myself}."
